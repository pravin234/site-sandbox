Relief Patrol PH


What is #reliefpatrolPH?

#reliefpatrolPH is an initiative that aims to guard the integrity of delivery of donations and all other resources intended for Typhoon Yolanda survivors and ensure that they are distributed properly. #reliefpatrolPH also aims to incite action by encouraging transparency in the deployment and distribution of foreign aid pledged to the government and in the operations of responsible relief organizations. It engages the public in verifying if dispatched donations has reached intended recipients. Through this initiative, we will create not just a community that is responsive and compassionate, but also one that is vigilant and accountable.

Objectives

  • Report. Monitor the receipt and delivery of relief goods and funds intended for Typhoon Yolanda survivors
  • Verify. For citizens to send feedback on whether relief operations, goods and funds have reached the communities in need; for citizens to be able to confirm the accuracy of reported information through a mobile platform (for transmitting to the online database)
  • Act. Spread the word: Laud effective use of the donations, or raise red flags for anomalous use of donations. Reports should be coordinated with the responsible relief organizations for appropriate action.
